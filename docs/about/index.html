What is Paws and Beaks all about?

ABOUT US

We take care of your best friend

Paws and Beaks is a professional company established in 2012 that follow the modern methods for pet care. We have one of the most advanced pet clinics in Europe as well as one of the most respected veterinarians not only on Europe, but in the whole world. What is more Paws and Beaks owns a very advanced SPA center, equipped with modern machinery, in order to make your favourite animal experience feelings that it has never experienced before.

Our team is currently working on developing our dog shelter and building more shelters for other animals. We try to save the homeless animals and provide them with everything they need, untill they find their new owner.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple: "An animal's life is worth as much as a human's life." For us animals deserve as much as we do, doesn't matter if it is health care, food, or just a little bit happyness. Pets are part of our families and are equal family members as we are, so they must enjoy everything we do isn't that right?

We are trying our best to teach people that having a pet is a big responsibility and it is not about feeding and walking, but is also about providing it with everything it needs to live happy and calm live.
