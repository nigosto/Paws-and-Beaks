ADOPTION

How to adopt your new friend?

After you have chosen your new pet from our gallery and filled in the simple form afterwards, you will need to wait for confirmation, based on complex algorithms. After you got a confirmation you will need to go to an office of Paws and Beaks, where you have to bring the required documents for adoption (you can check what documents you need at the FAQ section in contacts page).

If everything is going according to plan you can adopt your new friend right after you bring the needed documents. If you have any questions please contact us by sending us a message through the site or by email.
